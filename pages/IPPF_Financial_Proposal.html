4. Strategic Notes & Assumptions

Currency

All values are quoted in USD, strictly complying with the Terms of Reference templates.

Level of Effort Allocation

Indicative days total 216 across resources (Mihir Soni: 82 days, Harsh Jariwala: 70 days, Ranjith Gunasekaran: 64 days). Days are concentrated heavily in active design & build segments in Part 2. Final scopes are finalized at kickoff.

Cloud Hosting and Licenses

Hosting (Azure & Snowflake core capacity costs) is excluded. Per IPPF clarify briefings, infrastructure fees rest with IPPF, with our technical teams configuring integrations directly. Final licensing strategy is configured during Part 1.

VAT & GST Legal Status

Cost to IPPF: Services to IPPF are an export of services from India, which is zero-rated under Indian GST law. No GST, VAT or IGST is added — the quoted fee is the total amount payable.

Consultant's tax compliance: Any Indian tax registration or compliance is the consultant's sole responsibility, completed prior to invoicing, with no impact on IPPF's cost or payment process.

Affiliate licensing

Power BI licensing for Affiliate (MA/CP) end users is excluded, as the final user base and access model are defined during the needs analysis. We will recommend a scalable, costed model (e.g. Power BI Embedded or Premium Per User) in Part 1. Our team's own Power BI Pro development licences are included under Software & tools.
