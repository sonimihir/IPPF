Relevant Work Samples

A visual showcase of interactive Power BI dashboards delivered across public-sector, healthcare, and pharmaceutical domains. These samples demonstrate the capacity to build intuitive, scalable, and multi-domain analytics platforms.

Public-Sector Performance Monitoring

National Education Dashboard - MENFOP Djibouti

A comprehensive, fully French-language performance dashboard for the national education ministry. This platform monitors more than 400 schools across diverse operating contexts. It features a standardised performance-rating model, enabling comparative benchmarking and multi-level filtering. Designed for a central body monitoring decentralised units, making it a strong structural analogue to a federation Secretariat monitoring Affiliates.

Healthcare Service-Delivery

Nursing & Patient-Care Analytics - CarePlus+

An end-to-end analytics solution covering patient outcomes, quality and safety indicators, staffing ratios, and financial metrics. The dashboard is structured across role-specific views for clinical, operational, and management users. This work demonstrates direct experience with service-delivery indicators and the operational realities of a health-service-providing organisation.

Enterprise Data Architecture

Multi-Domain Pharmaceutical Platform

An enterprise pharmaceutical analytics platform spanning six functional domains: research and development, manufacturing, quality assurance, sales, and compliance. This engagement integrated diverse operational, financial, and regulatory data streams into a single coherent information architecture—directly relevant to managing multiple data sources (service delivery, finance, grants) within a unified Affiliate environment.
